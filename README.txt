This application system for both ordering and delivering groceries. 
Private individuals can register as deliverers/drivers and go shop and deliver groceries for
people that donâ€™t have the time or health to go shopping for themselves.  Shoppers can
register and publish their grocery lists, and the deliverers can than choose to "take" the
shopping task.

## To start the app
````
>npm install
>npm start
````

## To test the app on your phone
1. Download Expo app from app store or Google store
2. Scan the QR-code in the console
3. Watch the magic happen
